# Frequently Asked Questions

### What is a Regular Slotted Container (RSC)?

A Regular Slotted Container is the most common corrugated shipping box style, featuring four top flaps and four bottom flaps that meet in the center when closed.

### Can RSC boxes be manufactured in custom sizes?

Yes. Boxes can be produced in custom dimensions to match the products being packaged.

### Are different board strengths available?

Yes. Single-wall, double-wall, and other board grades are available depending on the weight and protection requirements of your shipment.
